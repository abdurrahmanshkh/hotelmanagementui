How Dynamic Pricing Works

Dynamic pricing automatically adjusts your room prices based on real-time occupancy. When occupancy is high (above 70%), prices increase to maximize revenue. When demand is low (below 30%), prices are slightly discounted to attract more bookings.

Low Demand (<30%) → Up to 15% Discount
Normal Demand (30%-70%) → Base Price
High Demand (>70%) → Up to 50% Premium
